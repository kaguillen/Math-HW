\begin{document}
Let $M = \zz^2$ and let $N$ be the $\zz$-submodule generated by the 2 elements 
\[\begin{pmatrix}
    120 \\ 240
\end{pmatrix} \ \text{ and } \ \begin{pmatrix}
    360 \\ -300
\end{pmatrix}\]

Find an element $f \in \text{Hom}_{\zz}(M,\zz)$ so that $f(N)$ is maximal in $\Sigma_{M,N}$. Where \[\Sigma_{M,N} = \set{(a_f) = \phi(N) \mid f \in \text{Hom}(M,\zz)}\] Prove that is is indeed maximal.
\end{document}
